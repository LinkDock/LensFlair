Founded in 2015, LensFlair has grown from a passionate solo photographer to a team of dedicated professionals committed to capturing life's most precious moments.

We believe in storytelling through imagery, creating photographs that evoke emotion and stand the test of time. Our approach combines technical expertise with artistic vision to deliver exceptional results for every client.
